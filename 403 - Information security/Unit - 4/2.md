# ISO 17799 Standard

## Introduction

**ISO 17799** is an international standard that provides **best practices and guidelines for Information Security Management**. It helps organizations protect their information assets from threats such as unauthorized access, data theft, and cyber attacks.

ISO 17799 was developed from BS 7799 and later became **ISO/IEC 27002**.

## Objectives of ISO 17799

* Protect information assets.
* Ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
* Reduce security risks.
* Establish effective security controls.
* Support business continuity.

## Major Security Domains of ISO 17799

### 1. Security Policy

* Defines the organization's security goals and rules.
* Provides direction for information security management.

### 2. Organization of Information Security

* Assigns security roles and responsibilities.
* Establishes a security management framework.

### 3. Asset Management

* Identifies and classifies information assets.
* Ensures proper protection of assets.

### 4. Human Resource Security

* Security measures before, during, and after employment.
* Includes employee awareness and training.

### 5. Physical and Environmental Security

* Protects buildings, equipment, and facilities.
* Prevents unauthorized physical access.

### 6. Communications and Operations Management

* Ensures secure operation of information systems.
* Includes backup, logging, and malware protection.

### 7. Access Control

* Restricts access to authorized users only.
* Uses passwords, authentication, and authorization mechanisms.

### 8. System Development and Maintenance

* Incorporates security into software development.
* Protects applications and databases.

### 9. Information Security Incident Management

* Provides procedures for reporting and handling security incidents.
* Helps minimize damage from attacks.

### 10. Business Continuity Management

* Ensures critical business operations continue during disasters.
* Includes disaster recovery planning.

### 11. Compliance

* Ensures compliance with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements.
* Supports audits and security reviews.

## Benefits of ISO 17799

* Improves information security.
* Reduces security risks and losses.
* Enhances customer trust.
* Helps meet legal requirements.
* Promotes security awareness among employees.

## Conclusion

ISO 17799 is a comprehensive standard that provides guidelines and best practices for managing information security. It helps organizations protect information assets, manage risks, and maintain secure business operations.
